About the Book

A revolutionary approach to understanding internships

The Internship Book is a first-of-its-kind multimedia guide created by teenagers, for teenagers. It aims to make internships more accessible, less intimidating, and genuinely useful for young people stepping into their first professional spaces.

Set to launch on 24th July 2025, this book will be written, illustrated, and managed by 23 teen contributors under the mentorship of the TeenInterns platform. It will explore what internships really are, why they are important, how to find the right fit, how to apply, and how to make the most of the experience.

It will also feature reflections, quotes, and real-world stories from professionals and industry leaders across fields, offering readers an inspiring blend of peer perspectives and expert advice.

Understanding the basics and exploring different types of opportunities.

Exploring the benefits and importance for your future career.

Step-by-step strategies to find, apply for, and succeed in internships.
