How to Use QR Codes in the Book

QR codes should be strategically placed throughout the book to enhance the reader's experience. Follow these guidelines:

  • Place QR codes at the end of relevant sections where additional content would be valuable
  • Include a brief description of what the reader will find when scanning the code
  • Make sure the QR code is large enough to be easily scanned (minimum 2.5cm √ó 2.5cm)
  • Test all QR codes before finalizing the book design
  • Consider adding a visual indicator or icon to differentiate content types (video, interactive content, etc.)

For the print version, download the PNG files and provide them to the design team. For the digital version, use the HTML examples provided in the templates.
